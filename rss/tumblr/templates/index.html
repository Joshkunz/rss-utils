Tumblr2Rss

tumblr2rss is a little script that turns your tumblr dashboard into an rss feed. Click the button below to get started. Note: using this service will effectively make your dashboard public, so use with caution.

Login with tumblr

How to Un-Register

You can unregister from this service (removing your ".rss" url) by going to "Apps" in your settings and clicking the "x" next to the name RSS-Utils Tumblr Bot
